Omo

  • github.com/omo-ai/omo
  • Overview
    • Open-source, AI-native enterprise search.
    • An LLM- and vector-store-agnostic platform for connecting workplace data sources and chatting with them — powering internal search, in-app Q&A, and customer-facing assistants.
    • Omo lets teams connect sources like Google Drive and Notion, then ask questions over that data with source citations. Designed API-first so the same backend can serve a chat UI, Slack, or custom clients.
  • Design
    • Built around pluggable LLMs, embedding models, and vector stores, with multi-tenancy and container/Kubernetes deployment for self-hosted or on-prem setups under Apache 2.0.
    • Self-service connectors such as Google Drive and Notion.
